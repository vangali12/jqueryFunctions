Hover over me to learn more about crickets

  1. Size of crickets depends on the species. They are usually 1 to 2 inches long.
  2. Crickets can be black, red, brown or green in color.
  3. Most species of crickets have flattened body and two pairs of membranous wings.
  4. Crickets have one pair of one inch long antennas, called feelers. Antennas can detect movement of the prey and facilitate finding of food.
